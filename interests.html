Games Reason
Baldur's Gate 3 One of the best, if not the best, game I've played. There's many classes and races you can choose. The story is very good and characters are very well written. This game has a lot of freedom and is fun to play with others.
Soulsborne Series My favorite video game series of all time, the Fromsoftware Souls series. I love replaying these games and doing challenge runs when replaying. The bosses of these games are some of the best bosses in video game history.
Red Dead 2 The best story game I've played. The graphics and visuals of this game are amazing. I love how realistic this game is. I love getting on this game to just ride on my horse, hunt animals, and fish.
